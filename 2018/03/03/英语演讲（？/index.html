英文：
The story takes place on a fictional farm where the maltreated animals rebel and overthrow their human overlords. They establish a seemingly utopian society where they work for and are governed by themselves; however, it doesn’t take long for the farm to deteriorate into a totalitarian state with a ruler who can only be described as a tyrant. The most pivotal factor responsible for this outcome is propaganda. Through the use of propaganda in the book, Orwell argues that a government’s power to control its people’s knowledge and views is that government’s capacity to manipulate and oppress.

中文：奥威尔讽刺了1917年的俄国革命以及随后数十年的极权主义苏联压迫。故事发生在一个虚构的农场，受虐待的动物反抗并推翻他们的人类上流社会。他们建立了一个看似乌托邦式的社会，在这裡他们为自己工作和自己管理; 然而，农场并不需要很长时间就会变成一个只能被描述为暴君的统治者极权主义国家。造成这一结果的最关键因素是宣传。奥威尔通过在书中使用宣传，认为政府控制其人民的知识和观点的权力是政府操纵和压迫的能力。
